Milestone 8

Volcanoes on the Four Corners States

For this milestone I chose to prepare a basemap for depicting the volcanoes located in the United States, 
both dormant and active. Specifically, I will focus on volcanoes located in the Four Corners states: New Mexico, Colorado, Utah and Arizona.
I've always been facinated by these structures and thought it would be a good concept and learning experience.
