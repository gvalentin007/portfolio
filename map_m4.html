Milestone 4

Best Beaches in Puerto Rico

For this milestone, I got a chance to play with the style options provided by the Google Maps APIs Styling Wizard.
Although for the concept that this milestone follows a satellite image is best for background, you can view the style changes
I made by clicking in "Map". Changes include color of the sea, natural and developed areas, natural parks and streets.
The map also includes markers for some of the best beaches in Puerto Rico, with a brief description.
